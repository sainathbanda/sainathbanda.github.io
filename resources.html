Data Sources & Databases
Essential datasets for agricultural, environmental & economic research
14 resources
💡

Tip for students: Always check if your institution has a subscription before paying. ICAR institutes often provide access to NSSO unit-level data and paid databases free of cost through ICAR-NAARM library.

🌾
ICRISAT Village Level Studies (VLS)
Long-term micro-level panel data on farm households from semi-arid tropics of India. Gold standard for rural household economics research.
FreeIndia
📋
NSSO / NSO Surveys
National Sample Survey data including Situation Assessment of Agricultural Households (SAS), Consumer Expenditure, and Employment surveys.
FreeIndia
🌍
FAO FAOSTAT
Comprehensive global database on food, agriculture, fisheries, and forestry. Covers production, trade, prices, emissions, and land use for 245+ countries.
FreeGlobal
🏦
World Bank Open Data
Free access to global development data — GDP, poverty, agriculture value added, rural population, food security indicators and more.
FreeGlobal
🗂️
India Agriculture Census
State and district-level data on landholdings, land use, irrigated area, and operational holdings. Crucial for farm-level structural analysis.
FreeIndia
🌱
DAC&FW — Area, Production & Yield
Ministry of Agriculture data on crop-wise area, production, and yield at state and all-India level. Essential for productivity and supply-side analysis.
FreeIndia
💰
AGMARKNET — Agricultural Prices
Daily market arrival and price data for agricultural commodities across mandis in India. Best source for commodity price analysis and market integration studies.
FreeIndia
🥛
NDDB — Dairy Statistics
National Dairy Development Board data on milk production, procurement, processing capacity, cattle population, and dairy cooperative performance.
FreeIndia
🌫️
GHG Emissions Data (EPA & UNFCCC)
Global greenhouse gas emissions inventories by sector. Useful for carbon farming, emission factor estimation, and climate policy research.
FreeGlobal
♻️
Verra Carbon Registry
Registry of verified carbon credits under the Verified Carbon Standard (VCS/VCM). Browse agricultural and forestry carbon projects globally and in India.
FreeGlobal
🔬
ICAR Open Data Portal
Research data from ICAR institutes across India covering crop varieties, livestock breeds, soil health, weather, and experimental trial data.
FreeIndia
📦
WDI R Package
Pull World Bank Development Indicators directly into R for analysis. Saves hours of manual downloading — just call WDI() with the indicator code.
FreeR Package
🗃️
Indiastat
Aggregated socio-economic and agricultural statistics for India at state and district level. Paid, but many ICAR and university libraries have subscriptions.
Paid/LibraryIndia
🌡️
CGIAR — CCAFS Data
Climate Change, Agriculture and Food Security program datasets. Includes climate-smart agriculture interventions, farm surveys, and GHG measurement protocols.
FreeGlobal

Software & Tools
Analytical, econometric, and productivity tools for researchers
12 resources
💡

Recommendation: Start with R + RStudio for econometrics and Python for data wrangling. Both are free, widely used, and have massive communities. STATA is powerful but expensive — check if your institute provides a license.

📈
R + RStudio
The go-to free software for econometrics in agricultural research. Packages like plm, lme4, AER, ggplot2, and fixest cover almost every model you'll need.
FreeOpen Source
🐍
Python (Anaconda Distribution)
Excellent for data cleaning, time series (Prophet, ARIMA), machine learning, and automation. pandas + statsmodels + scikit-learn cover most agri-economics use cases.
FreeOpen Source
📊
STATA
Industry-standard for microeconometrics and panel data. Excellent for IV, DiD, PSM, and impact evaluation. Many ICAR/university licenses available.
Paid/License
⚙️
GAMS — CGE Modelling
General Algebraic Modelling System — used for Computable General Equilibrium (CGE) models. Essential for trade policy and economy-wide impact analysis.
Paid/License
📚
Zotero — Reference Manager
Free and open-source reference manager. Organise papers, generate citations automatically, and sync across devices. Essential for thesis writing.
FreeOpen Source
🗺️
QGIS — Spatial Analysis
Free GIS software for spatial analysis of agricultural land use, irrigation coverage, and environmental mapping. Increasingly important in agri-economics.
FreeOpen Source
🕸️
VOSviewer — Bibliometric Analysis
Free tool for constructing and visualising bibliometric networks — co-authorship, co-citation, keyword co-occurrence. Great for systematic review papers.
Free
📉
bibliometrix (R Package)
Comprehensive R-based framework for science mapping and bibliometric analysis. Works with Web of Science and Scopus exports.
FreeR Package
📡
EViews — Time Series
Widely used for time series econometrics — ARIMA, VAR, VECM, ARCH/GARCH models. Popular in agricultural price analysis and forecasting research.
Paid/License
🤖
AI Writing & Coding Assistants
ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini can help debug R/Python code, improve academic writing, and brainstorm research frameworks. Use critically — always verify outputs.
Freemium
🔗
Connected Papers
Visual tool to explore literature around a seed paper. Instantly see related work, prior art, and derivative papers. Excellent for literature review mapping.
Free
🗄️
Mendeley
Reference manager and academic social network by Elsevier. Useful for organizing PDFs, annotating papers, and discovering new literature in your field.
Free

Key Journals
High-quality journals to read, follow, and target for publication
12 journals
💡

For ICAR scientists: NAAS score matters for your annual performance assessment. Focus on journals with NAAS ≥ 6.0 for significant credit. Check the NAAS website for the latest updated ratings.

🌾
Agricultural Economics (IAAE)
Top-tier international journal of the IAAE. Covers all areas of agricultural economics — production, markets, trade, development, environment.
GlobalSubscription
🏘️
Journal of Rural Studies
Interdisciplinary journal on rural societies, economies, and environments. Excellent for smallholder agriculture, rural livelihoods, and policy papers.
Global
🌿
Ecological Economics
Leading journal for ecosystem services valuation, environmental policy, and sustainability economics. High relevance for carbon farming and NRE research.
Global
🏭
Journal of Environmental Management
Broad scope covering environmental policy, natural resource management, and sustainability. Good fit for externality cost studies and carbon credit research.
Global
🍚
Food Policy
Policy-oriented research on food systems, price transmission, food security, and agricultural markets. Popular among IFPRI and CGIAR researchers.
Global
🇮🇳
Agricultural Economics Research Review
AESA's flagship Indian journal. Highly relevant for domestic agri-economics research. Good target journal for India-focused studies. NAAS 5.15.
IndiaOpen Access
📗
Indian Journal of Agricultural Economics
Official journal of the Indian Society of Agricultural Economics. One of the oldest and most respected agri-economics journals in India.
India
🏗️
Agriculture and Human Values
Interdisciplinary journal on social, ethical, and human dimensions of agriculture and food systems. Good for qualitative and mixed-methods work.
Global
🌏
Journal of Agribusiness in Developing & Emerging Economies
Emerald journal focused on agribusiness in developing countries. IF 2.3 — covers value chains, market access, and innovation in agriculture. (IF 2.3)
Global
♻️
Frontiers in Sustainable Food Systems
Open-access journal covering sustainable food systems, agroecology, and climate-smart agriculture. NAAS 9.10 — increasingly popular for interdisciplinary work.
Open AccessGlobal
🔭
Current Science
Prestigious multidisciplinary Indian journal published by IAS. High NAAS score (7.0). Good for broad-reach papers on agriculture, environment, and policy.
IndiaOpen Access

NAAS Journal Ratings Portal
Official NAAS portal to check current ratings of all journals. Essential reference for ICAR scientists before submitting manuscripts. Updated annually.
FreeIndia

ICAR's prestigious research fellowships for M.Sc. and Ph.D. students. JRF provides ₹31,000/month and SRF ₹35,000/month with HRA. Highly competitive — worth every effort.
IndiaGovt. Funded

CGIAR centers (CIMMYT, IRRI, IFPRI, ICRISAT etc.) regularly hire postdocs and young professionals in agricultural economics. Excellent career launch pad for international exposure.

Global

Methods & Tutorials
Step-by-step guides for econometric methods used in agricultural economics
10 resources
💡

Learning path suggestion: Start with OLS → Panel Data → IV/2SLS → PSM/DiD in that order. Master each before moving on. Nick Huntington-Klein's free book The Effect is the best modern introduction to causal inference for economists.

📘
The Effect — Causal Inference (Free Book)
Nick Huntington-Klein's free online textbook on causal inference and research design. Covers DiD, IV, RDD, PSM with R and Stata code. The best modern intro to impact evaluation.
FreeOnline Book
🎵
Causal Inference: The Mixtape (Free)
Scott Cunningham's free book on causal inference — DAGs, potential outcomes, DiD, RDD, synthetic control. Very readable with real examples. Highly recommended for impact evaluation work.
FreeOnline Book
📈
Econometrics with R (Free Textbook)
Free online textbook covering econometrics using R — OLS, heteroskedasticity, panel data, IV regression. Perfect for students transitioning from STATA to R.
FreeR
🗂️
Library of Statistical Techniques (LOST)
Searchable library showing how to run any statistical technique in R, Python, STATA, and SAS side by side. Incredibly useful when switching between software.
FreeReference
▶️
YouTube — Econometrics & Causal Inference
Nick Huntington-Klein's YouTube lectures on causal inference, panel data, and research design. Clear, practical, and free. Supplement with Ben Lambert's econometrics series.
FreeVideo
📦
CRAN Econometrics Task View
Curated list of all R packages for econometrics organised by method — time series, panel data, IV, survival analysis, spatial econometrics. Your one-stop R package reference.
FreeR
🔀
Propensity Score Methods Guide
Comprehensive resource on propensity score matching and weighting methods. Essential for impact evaluation of agricultural interventions, schemes, and technology adoption studies.
Free
📗
Impact Evaluation in Practice (World Bank)
Free World Bank textbook on impact evaluation methods — RCT, RDD, DiD, IV. Widely used in development economics. Downloadable PDF, very practical with policy examples.
FreeTextbook
🐣
R for Data Science (Free Book)
Hadley Wickham's free book for learning data wrangling, visualisation, and analysis in R using tidyverse. The standard starting point for anyone learning R for research.
FreeR
⚙️
CGE Modelling — Beginner's Guide
Introduction to Computable General Equilibrium modelling using GEMPACK. Essential starting resource if you want to do economy-wide policy analysis like trade shock simulations.
FreeGuide

Must-Read Books
Essential reading list for agricultural & environmental economists
12 books
💡

Reading strategy: Don't try to read everything cover to cover. Learn to read research books by abstract → introduction → conclusion → methods. Build your own annotated reading list in Zotero as you go.

🌾
Agricultural Economics — Penson, Capps & Rosson
The standard undergraduate and postgraduate textbook for agricultural economics. Covers production, supply, demand, markets, trade, and policy. Essential foundation reading.
Textbook
🌿
Natural Resource & Environmental Economics — Perman et al.
The definitive NRE textbook. Covers environmental valuation, externalities, pollution policy, natural resource depletion, and sustainability economics. Core reading for your specialisation.
Textbook
💡
Poor Economics — Banerjee & Duflo
Nobel Prize-winning economists explain what works in fighting poverty using randomised controlled trials. Highly readable and directly relevant to smallholder agriculture research.
Popular
🌐
Good Economics for Hard Times — Banerjee & Duflo
Addresses trade, immigration, inequality, and climate change through the lens of development economics. Accessible and highly relevant for understanding agricultural trade policy.
Popular
📜
Mostly Harmless Econometrics — Angrist & Pischke
The bible of modern applied econometrics. Covers IV, DiD, RDD, and panel data with clarity and wit. Essential for anyone doing empirical agricultural economics research.
Methods
🌳
Valuing Environmental Preferences — Bateman et al.
Comprehensive guide to stated and revealed preference methods for environmental valuation — contingent valuation, choice experiments, hedonic pricing. Directly relevant to your ecosystem services work.
Methods
📊
Introduction to Econometrics — Stock & Watson
The most widely taught econometrics textbook globally. Covers regression, time series, panel data, and IV with clear intuition. Perfect companion to your STATA or R learning.
Textbook
🕊️
Development as Freedom — Amartya Sen
Sen's landmark book on development economics — capabilities, freedoms, and human development. Essential philosophical grounding for rural development and food security research.
Classic
🌏
Governing the Commons — Elinor Ostrom
Nobel Prize-winning work on how communities manage shared natural resources without privatisation or top-down regulation. Foundational reading for NRE, water management, and CPR research.
Classic
♻️
Carbon Markets & Climate Finance — Literature
Start with Stern Review on Climate Change (free PDF) + IPCC reports on agriculture. These are the foundational policy documents for carbon farming and agricultural emissions research.
Free PDFsPolicy
🍽️
The Omnivore's Dilemma — Michael Pollan
Accessible exploration of modern food systems, industrial agriculture, and sustainability. Great for developing a broader perspective on the political economy of food. Recommended leisure reading.
Popular
🌡️
The Stern Review on Climate Change (Free)
The landmark UK government report on economics of climate change by Nicholas Stern. Free PDF available online. Essential background reading for anyone working on carbon markets and climate policy.
Free PDFPolicy
