About Me

I am a Scientist (ICAR-ARS) in the Division of Socio-Economics and Extension, ICAR Research Complex for Eastern Region, Patna, Bihar. My work sits at the intersection of natural resource economics, environmental policy, and sustainable agriculture.

My Ph.D. research focuses on the "Economic analysis of externalities of urban and peri-urban dairies in Hyderabad" at ICAR-National Dairy Research Institute, Karnal.

Prior to ICAR, I gained industry experience as an Associate Manager at ITC (campus recruitment), and my M.Sc. research at UAS Bengaluru focused on estimating groundwater and surface water costs and use efficiency under the Godavari Command Area.

Focus Areas

Research Interests

🌱
Carbon Farming & Green Credits
Designing voluntary carbon credit frameworks for smallholder farmers; evaluating financial viability and policy effectiveness of carbon markets in India.
🌿
Ecosystem Services
Multi-dimensional valuation of ecosystem services and disservices from agricultural systems, including livestock and indigenous breeds.
💧
Environmental Impact Assessment
Estimating externality costs of irrigation and urban dairies; analysing negative externalities on farmer profitability and natural resources.
🐄
Livestock Economics
Economic analysis of dairy systems, milk flow patterns, livestock product consumption trends, and policy implications for the dairy sector.
📊
Trade & Policy Modelling
Computable General Equilibrium (CGE) modelling of trade policy shocks; distributional consequences of tariffs on India's agro-economy.
🌾
Smallholder Agriculture
Market integration, marketing channel choice, and socio-economic implications of climate stress for resource-poor farmers in Eastern India.
